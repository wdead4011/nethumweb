Our Proud History

A legacy of excellence and service to the community

Founding Years

Balalla National College was established in the early 20th century with a mission to provide quality education to the rural youth of Balalla and surrounding areas. Originally founded as a small temple-based school, it later evolved into a formal government school through the efforts of local educationists and community leaders.

Notable Principals

Over the decades, Balalla National College has flourished under the leadership of dedicated principals. From the founding principal Mr. K.D. Herath to the present, each principal contributed to the development of infrastructure, student discipline, and academic success.

Legacy & Future Vision

Today, Balalla National College stands as a symbol of pride for the Kurunegala District. Our students excel in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities. We continue to focus on innovation, values-based education, and global readiness.
